Our Culinary Journey

The story of our evolution, so far.

Our little spaceship, designed to follow the course set by nature, has completed its first full lap around the sun (yup, Locavore NXT is now over a year old). And here we find ourselves... Evolving.

Like any other living organism (or bold idea), we're growing through stages, getting stronger, a bit more mature, and finding new ways to express our ideas. On many levels, we are unrecognisable from where we were only a year ago, both in how we feel about the progress of the menu itself and also the way nature is reclaiming our space.

The habitat is rich and diverse, the bees have plenty of flowers to enjoy, and the rooftop Food Forest is established and acts as our 'living calendar', marking the rhythms of the hyper-local seasons.

And so, this brings us full circle to the new season's tasting menu; Nature's Compass 2.0. You could say that we're starting to bud and flower.
